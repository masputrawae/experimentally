## Math Equations (LaTeX / MathJax)

### Inline Math

Euler's formula: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$

Pythagorean theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$

### Block Math

$$
\int_{a}^{b} x^2 \,dx = \frac{b^3}{3} - \frac{a^3}{3}
$$

$$
F(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!} (x - a)^n
$$

Quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$
