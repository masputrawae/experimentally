## Project Overview
Led the implementation of a comprehensive ERP system for ManufactureX Inc., integrating all business processes including inventory, supply chain, finance, and HR into a unified platform. The system was designed to improve operational efficiency and provide real-time business insights.

## Key Features
- Modular microservices architecture
- Real-time data synchronization
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Role-based access control
- Integration with legacy systems

## Challenges
- Managing complex business workflows
- Ensuring data consistency across modules
- Handling high transaction volumes
- Training employees on new system

## Results
- 30% improvement in operational efficiency
- 20% reduction in inventory costs
- Real-time visibility into business operations
- 99.9% system reliability achieved
