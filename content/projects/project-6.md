## Project Overview
Developed a modern e-commerce platform for ShopSphere Inc. that provides a seamless shopping experience across web and mobile devices. The platform features advanced product search, personalized recommendations, and secure payment processing.

## Key Features
- Advanced product search and filtering
- Personalized product recommendations
- Secure payment gateway integration
- Real-time inventory management
- Multi-language and currency support

## Challenges
- Handling high traffic during sales events
- Implementing complex product search algorithms
- Ensuring PCI compliance for payment processing
- Managing large product catalog efficiently

## Results
- 40% increase in conversion rate
- 3 second average page load time
- 99.5% uptime during peak sales
- 4.9/5 customer satisfaction rating
